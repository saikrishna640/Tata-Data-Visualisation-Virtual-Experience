# Tata Group — Data Visualisation Virtual Experience
**Platform:** Forage | **Duration:** Sep 2025 – Oct 2025

## Overview
Four-task data visualisation simulation framing and presenting 
business insights for C-suite decision makers at a global retail company.

## Task 1 — Business Question Framing
Drafted 8 strategic questions for CEO and CMO from a retail dataset:
- CEO focus: revenue trends, international expansion potential, 
  demand forecasting, inventory strategy
- CMO focus: customer lifetime value, loyalty program performance, 
  demographic targeting, retention by product category

## Task 2 — Chart Selection
Matched business questions to optimal chart types:
- Time series → Line chart
- Country comparison → Bar chart with dual axis (quantity + revenue)
- Customer ranking → Sorted bar chart descending
- Regional demand → Treemap / filled map

## Task 3 — Tableau Dashboard (4 Charts)
Built on cleaned retail dataset (removed negative quantities and 
sub-zero unit prices before analysis):
- Sheet 1: 2011 monthly revenue time series for CEO forecasting
- Sheet 2: Top 10 countries by revenue + quantity (excluding UK)
- Sheet 3: Top 10 customers by revenue, highest to lowest
- Sheet 4: Regional demand treemap across all countries (excluding UK)

## Task 4 — Executive Presentation
Developed and delivered a 5-minute data presentation to simulated 
CEO and CMO audience covering all four analytical findings.

## Tools Used
Tableau, Microsoft Excel, Data Cleaning, Business Intelligence
